All kindness project


Introduction:

All kindness is an aggregation platform powered by a recommendation system engine that helps people to find the right projects for them and provide the information about different directions for donations. This project is developed in the context of the Social Impact Data Hack 2017. More info: http://sidh2017.ut.ee

Our blog: Team all kindness

Project steps:

  • Creating a poll to understand the people preferences
  • Analyze obtained data, ordering the preferences
  • Filter the dataset of charity projects regarding the preferences
  • Create web-project application

Future prospects:

  • We are going to save the information about people's preferences and their final choices to donate. It can be used later to propose projects even without not sufficient information about person. Recommendation system can be upgraded after obtaining the sufficient volume of data.
  • Collected information can also be used to understand patterns in how people search for charity programs and opportunities to help and use this information to give feedback to NGOs on how they can better advertise their work.
